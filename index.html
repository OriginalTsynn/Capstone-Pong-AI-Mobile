Pong
Instructions

Welcome to Pong. A classic game, with a couple new twists!
Originally, Pong was played on just the Y-axis (up and down)
This version, however, also incorporates the X-axis (left and right)
Player 1 moves using WASD
Player 2 uses up down left right (when enabled).
Player 1 will get a point when they get the ball to hit the right boundary wall.
Player 2 will get a point when they get the ball to hit the left boundary wall.
Go have fun!
